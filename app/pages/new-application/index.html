APPLICATION for new artists

This is a one-time application for all artists that are either new to the studio tour, or have been on the studio tour but are applying to show in a new medium. If you are accepted onto the tour, you will then need to set up your member account on this website and submit a yearly member contract (it’s online through the Members page and is called My Contract.) This year’s member contract is due by March 2, 2025.
